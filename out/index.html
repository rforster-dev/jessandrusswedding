Near by

Things to do

If you are planning on visiting the local area, here are some suggestions for things to do.

Westonbirt Arboretum

Featuring over 2,500 different species of trees and 600 acres of woodland, this arboretum is right next door to our wedding venue. Walk on up into the sky with the treetop bridge, and watch the foliage spring into life.

Highgrove House and Gardens

Highgrove is the private residence of King Charles III, who opens up the gardens every year between April and October. Having dedicated more than 40 years to maintaining it, the gardens offer plenty of flora and fauna to explore. -

Westonbirt Golf Course

If you fancy bringing your clubs with you, this course is set within the beautiful grounds of Westonbirt School. Russ is a regular visitor, even if he did almost hit a ball through his windscreen in the car park that one time…

Tetbury

A lovely town with lots of independent shops, antique dealers, cafés and restaurants.

Malmesbury

A small, historic town with a fascinating Benedictine abbey (it's the resting place of King Æthelstan, the first King of England, who died in 939 AD), and a variety of independent shops, cafés and pubs.

Sherston

Visit our home from home. Our little Cotswolds village is just down the road from our wedding venue. We're biased, but it boasts afantastic pub, a medieval church, and somerather nice cafés. There are also lots oflovely walksaround the area if you fancy stretching your legs and seeing the North Wiltshire countryside.
